Whitefang

Path: NPWiki / Items / Whitefang

Whitefang is a unique long blade, two handed sword. It was previously obtainable from the New Life Festival Event from the weapons chest.

It looks the same as the Nordic Claymore.

A player holding Whitefang.

Special effects

While a player has Whitefang equipped, a semi-transparent snow wolf with the name of White Fang will be summoned.
