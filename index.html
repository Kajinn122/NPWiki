About us

We are the official, community driven, Nerevarine Prophecies Wikipedia.

The main goal of the Nerevarine Prophecies Wikipedia is to gather information regarding items, players, updates, lore or other curiosities that players have about the server, and make them easy to access for anyone, at any time.

Nerevarine Prophecies is a Tes3MP server made by Learwolf. It is a vanilla server, meaning that no other additional mods are required to join. Despite the fact that it is a vanilla server, there are still multiple vanilla friendly features, such as: jewelry crafting, tailoring, housing, perks system, and much more.

While it is important to note the features the server has to offer, custom items have a significant impact on the gameplay, making this a more pleasant and interesting experience for both new and old Morrowind players.
